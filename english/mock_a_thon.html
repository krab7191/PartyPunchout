PARTY PUNCHOUT MOCK-A-THON


We all know that Washington is broken: government shutdowns.. big-money influence.. extreme partisanship.. unwillingness to compromise.. politicians more loyal to their own party than to their constituents.

This is your chance to make your voice heard! Join the Party Punchout Video Mock-A-Thon and show how ridiculous things have gotten in Washington — it’s the first step toward taking our democracy back!

Send us your own video pointing out a dumb action or stupid, inane statement by a politician (or other public personality). We all love our country and our democracy — but individual politicians and public figures are fair game.. so let’s point out their idiocies! And if your video is one of the first 100 uploaded, we’ll send you a Party Punchout T-shirt.

If you’re a professional videographer, this is a way for your work to be seen by lots of people. Even if you’re not, it’s still a way for you to vent, get your ideas out there — and get an extremely limited edition T-shirt — one of only 100 produced world-wide.

JOIN THE MOVEMENT

Here’s how: Make a video of any type: animated, live-action, — anything goes. You can dub-over or add text to, or otherwise alter, an actual news broadcast (just make sure you observe copyright laws! https://www.youtube.com/yt/copyright/). You can draw your own cartoons or do clay animation. You can link still-shots together with captions. You can use issues in the bubbles in our game app, Party Punchout (see links at bottom of page). It can be as simple or as complex as your imagination allows. In fact, the sky’s the limit!


Here are the complete guidelines for the submission:

  1. Your video MUST comply with all the standard YouTube terms and conditions (https://www.youtube.com/static?template=terms).
  2. Your video MUST comply with all YouTube’s community standards (https://www.youtube.com/t/community_guidelines). No porn, graphic violence, etc. etc.
  3. Your video may NOT use or refer to any topic related to the war in the Middle East. We want to remain focused on American politics here in America!
  4. Your video quality MUST be 720p resolution or better.
  5. Audio must be of high quality.
  6. Your video MUST be submitted to this site only, and not used for or submitted to any contest, competition or for any commercial use.

IMPORTANT: THIS IS HOW TO SUBMIT YOUR VIDEO!

  1. You need to have your own personal YouTube channel. It’s easy to set up! Go to https://www.youtube.com/create_channel to set up your channel.
  2. After completing your video, upload it to YOUR OWN YouTube channel. Important: MARK IT AS "UNLISTED" If it is marked ‘public’ or ‘private,’ it will be rejected.
  3. After the video has been uploaded and marked as ‘unlisted’ — click “Share — unlisted,” then copy the video URL, paste it into an email and send it to video@partypunchout.com.
  4. Include the TITLE of the video, and your own name or screen name, or however you would like to be identified.
  5. We will review videos for acceptability and then upload them into the Party Punchout channel — with your name, attribution / identifier intact. We believe in giving credit where credit is due!
  6. If your video is one of the first 100 we upload to our channel — not only will you get the recognition (who knows, you could end up famous!) — we’ll send you a follow-up email requesting a shipping address, and we’ll send you a limited-edition Party Punchout T-shirt! (This will be a limited edition print run with fixed numbers of each size….send your video in early to ensure getting the size you request!)

You MUST follow these procedures or your video may be rejected. Party Punchout decisions regarding compliance with these procedures is final.

GOOD LUCK — and here’s to a saner political scene in America!
